ICE-CREAMS

Mangobar

Mangalore's iconic ice cream brand, Mangobär, offers a unique and delicious experience. With a wide range of flavors inspired by local fruits and ingredients, Mangobär's ice creams are a must-try when visiting Mangalore. From classic flavors like mango and coconut to innovative combinations like jackfruit and tender coconut, Mangobär's ice creams are a perfect blend of traditional and modern flavors.
